How You Can Help Make Mental Health More Accessible

Destigmatize Mental Health

Often the barrier which keeps people from recieving help, is the fear of judgement, unfortunatly this is often a valid fear as there is stigma and misunderstandings on mental health conditions. Here are some things you can do to help solve the problem:

-Educate yourself on mental health conditions.
-Listen to and support those around you who may have a mental health condition
-Don't be afraid to correct others when you hear a misconception about mental illnesses.
